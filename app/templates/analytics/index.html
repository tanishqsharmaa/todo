How the AI Works

Priority Prediction

Combines deadline urgency (30%), importance (25%), postponement history (20%), estimation accuracy (15%), and task age (10%) to calculate a dynamic 0-100 priority score.

Focus Time Analysis

Analyzes when you complete tasks most efficiently and suggests optimal time windows for different types of work based on your historical patterns.

Sentiment-Aware Reminders

Adjusts reminder tone based on detected stress levels in your task descriptions and daily reflections. High stress = gentler reminders.
